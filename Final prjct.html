New Prince Shri Bhavani Arts and Science College was established in 1999 by Smt. Abaranjee Ammal Educational Society which also runs several educational institutions and has been serving the society for more than three decades. The college is affiliated to the prestigious Madras University.

The College has a prestigious 20 years of excellence in holistic education as of 2020 and this temple of learning has been striving untiringly for the growth of students. The institution is led by visionary management with strong exposure to industry and academics.

VISION
To be a student-centric academic institution that stands by its motto "Discipline, Devotion, and Determination" and strives to impact value-based education and to inculcate the latest trends to create innovators and to attain wisdom through holistic education.


MISSION
Aiming for academic excellence, knowledge enhancement, and critical thinking capabilities. To uplift the rural youth, innovative and dynamic teaching is adopted. With the spirit of skill, charisma, faith, creativity, discipline, respect, and social service etiquette, an optimistic approach is taught.
